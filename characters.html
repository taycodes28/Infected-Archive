Characters

Explore the characters of One Dark Window.

Protagonist

Elspeth Spindle

A young woman hiding a dangerous secret — a monster called the Nightmare living inside her mind. Cunning and determined, she will do whatever it takes to protect herself and those she loves.

"The two were together, so the two were the same: "The girl, the King...and the monster they became."

Protagonist

Ravyn Yew

Captain of the Destriers and eldest of the Yew family. Stern and duty-bound, Ravyn carries the weight of his family's secrets while enforcing the king's brutal laws.

"It is not they who bring the reckoning, Ravyn. It is us."

Supporting character

Emory

A Destrier who rides alongside Ravyn, loyal and steadfast. Emory is the kind of person who says little but notices everything, and his quiet presence is felt long after he leaves a room.

"There was something strange about Emory Yew. Now I understood what it really was. The infection-it was eating at him, ripping away his sanity."

Supporting character

Ione

Elspeth's dearest friend, gentle and quietly perceptive. Ione carries a grace that masks a deeper strength, and her loyalty to those she loves runs deeper than anyone realizes.

"She didn't pretend, Ione. She simply was."

Ally

Jespyr Yew

Ravyn's spirited sister, warm and perceptive where her brother is cold. Jespyr sees through people in a way that makes her both an invaluable ally and a dangerous enemy to deceive.

"Be wary. Be clever. Be good."

Enigmatic

The Shepherd King

An ancient and legendary figure whose story is woven into the very origins of the Mirrorveil and the Providence Cards. More myth than man — or so most believe.

"Nothing is free. Nothing is safe. Magic is love, but also, it's hate. It comes at a cost. Your found, and your lost. Magic is love, but also, it's hate."

Supporting character

Elm

The youngest of the Yew brothers, Elm is sharp-tongued and irreverent — a stark contrast to Ravyn's rigidity. Beneath his careless exterior lies someone far more observant and conflicted than he lets on.

"Better distrustful than delusional."

Antagonist

Hauth Yew

The younger Yew brother and the king's ruthless enforcer. Cold and calculating, Hauth uses his Black card without mercy against any who threaten the crown.

"The High Prince was a brute. Bathed in the red light of his Scythe Card, Haulth had no qualms forcing others to do his bidding..."

Antagonist

King Rowan

The paranoid ruler of Blunder whose obsession with Providence Cards has poisoned the kingdom. He rules through fear, executing the infected while secretly coveting their power.

"His cards are but weapons, his Kingdom now cruel. Shepard of folly, King of the fools."
