Magic System

In the kingdom of Blunder, magic is not a gift. It is a curse.

The Nightmare

The Nightmare is an ancient spirit who lives inside Elspeth's mind, born from the Mirrorveil — the magical infection that spreads from the Twisted Wood. He is not simply a voice or a feeling. He is a presence, aware and calculating, with his own desires and agenda. The Nightmare is what makes Elspeth different from every other infected person in Blunder. While others carry Providence Cards and hear faint whispers, Elspeth carries something far older and more powerful. The Nightmare can see through her eyes, speak through her thoughts, and when the infection deepens — he can take control of her body entirely. This is both her greatest weapon and her greatest fear. Every time she leans on his power, she risks losing herself to him. The line between Elspeth and the Nightmare grows thinner the more she uses what he offers.

The Nightmare is ancient, cunning, and dangerous — and he lives inside Elspeth's mind.

Providence Cards

Providence Cards are the most common form of magic in Blunder. They are small, enchanted cards that infected people carry with them. Each card has a unique power, and they can be used for a variety of purposes — from healing wounds to manipulating emotions to glimpsing the future. However, using Providence Cards comes with a cost. The more you rely on them, the more you become dependent on the infection. Over time, the cards can consume your mind and soul, leaving you a hollow shell of your former self.

Providence Cards are powerful, but they come with a heavy price. The more you use them, the more you lose yourself to the infection. There are in total 12 cards.

The Black Horse

The Black Horse is a card of combat. When activated, it enhances the user's physical abilities, granting them increased strength, speed, and agility for a short duration. However, prolonged use can lead to aggressive behavior and a loss of control.
