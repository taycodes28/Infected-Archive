One Dark Window

A tribute to one of my favorite books. A dark fantasy set in a cursed kingdom where a young woman hides a
dangerous secret — a monster living in her mind. To save her world she must make a deal with the very thing she fears most.

My Reading Story

For weeks this book was on my Goodreads "Want to Read" list. It had good reviews and I was interested in reading it. My family and I were preparing for our first cruise. This means we were constantly questioning what to bring and first on my list was a book. I know when most people think of vacation, reading is not the first thing that comes to mind. However, for me, it is the perfect opportunity to dive into a new story and escape reality for a while.
